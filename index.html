IMPACT Lab is directed by Zhiming Cui in the school of Biomedical Engineering at ShanghaiTech University.

Zhiming Cui is an Assistant Professor in the School of Biomedical Engineering at the ShanghaiTech University. He received his Ph.D. degree from Department of Computer Science at the University of Hong Kong in 2022. His research interests include medical image analysis, deep learning, and 3D vision. Particularly, his current research focuses on advanced medical imaging computing algorithms, digital dentistry, and medical image reconstruction etc. His work has been published on several top journals and conferences, including Nature Communications, TMI, MedIA, TNNLS, MICCAI, CVPR, ECCV, etc.

We sit at the intersection of medical imaging computing, image reconstruction (e.g., CBCT, DSA, and PET/CT), and digital dentistry, striving to improve the quality of medical diagnostics and patient care through innovative technology. We have made key contributions in these areas, notably developing algorithms for precise medical image analysis and advancing techniques in real-world.

Research Interests: Advanced Medical Imaging Computing, Medical Image Reconstruction, AI in Digital Dentistry.
